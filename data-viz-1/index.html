A curious dip into data visualization

22 November 2023

It's been a while since I wrote an update here. I don't make it a rule to update this website at a regular interval but I do think it's nice to reflect on certain topics, from time to time.

Context

I've been so busy with the exciting ambitious projects at work, and the exciting ambitious projects on my personal life like moving house and converting a campervan, I haven't had a lot of bandwidth for playing with tech projects at home.

One thing I have recently been looking at, almost as a creative endeavour more so than a technical project, has been data visualization tasks. I call them tasks because they are much more finite and single-purpose than a typical software project.

I've always had a soft spot for pretty graphs and different ways to visualise and understand data. I can't quite tell where it originated from, but I know the book 'Factfulness' really fostered my appreciation for the art of story telling with data.

I will share a few of the graphs separately. I might try to expand a bit more on my journey to generate these graphs. Although I must say for a great deal of these, I simply picked an interesting dataset, went through the different ways of plotting data and chose what I felt could represent it best (or in an interesting way at least).
